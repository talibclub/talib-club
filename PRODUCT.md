# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Thai-speaking Muslims and students of knowledge who want to read academic Islamic articles, download PDF books, listen to lectures, study the history of Islamic scholars, and track book orders from Talib Publisher.

## Product Purpose
An academic Islamic portal for Talib Club, providing a curated directory of articles, books, media, and scholar timelines, designed to make Islamic knowledge accessible, comfortable to read, and modern.

## Brand Personality
Academic (วิชาการ), Comfortable to the eyes (ดูสบายตา), Inviting/Attractive (น่าเข้า). It should feel trustworthy, intellectual, calm, and highly readable.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered news portals with excessive ads, flashing elements, and tight text line heights.
- Boring, flat, default-bootstrap academic sites that lack visual polish and modern typography.
- Saturated SaaS landing pages with huge marketing buzzwords and aggressive gradient text.

## Design Principles
- **Reading Comfort First**: Prioritize generous line spacing, high contrast, clean typography (Prompt/sans-serif), and a comfortable reading width (max-width 70ch) for long-form articles.
- **Academic Serenity**: Use calm, muted, natural color palettes (soft greens/teals and warm/cool off-whites) that reduce eye strain and feel intellectual yet welcoming.
- **Structured Taxonomy**: Present scholars, books, and articles in clean, well-spaced layouts (avoiding nested grids or complex tables on small screens) with clear badges and filters.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Level AA contrast for body text (especially body text in reading mode).
- Proper dark/light mode toggle with smooth transitions and persistent state.
- Highly legible font sizes and scale for users of all ages.
